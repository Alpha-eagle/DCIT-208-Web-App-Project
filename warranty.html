Warranty Information

At Westrock Enterprise, we stand behind the quality of our tires. We offer a comprehensive warranty program that covers our tires for a certain period of time or mileage, whichever comes first.

Warranty Coverage

  • 5-year limited warranty on all passenger tires
  • 6-year limited warranty on all light truck tires
  • 7-year limited warranty on all commercial tires

Warranty Claims

To make a warranty claim, please contact our customer service department at warranty@westrockenterprise.com or call us at 1-800-WESTROCK.
